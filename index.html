PM4KNIME

State-of-the-art process mining in KNIME Analytics Platform

PM4KNIME is a process mining extension, developed by the process mining research group of Fraunhofer FIT, for the leading open-source platform, to data science solutions, KNIME Analytics Platform. PM4KNIME implements several state-of-the-art process mining algorithms in a user-friendly manner and is free of charge for non-commercial and academic use.

KNIME is designed to compose and execute a sequence of data manipulation steps, namely a workflow, in scientific applications. By combining this with the process mining domain, it enables the support of large-scale experiments in process mining.

As a community extension for KNIME, PM4KNIME integrates the Process Mining tools from ProM into the KNIME Analytics Platform. Classical plugins from ProM are wrapped as node extensions in KNIME and can be used to build a complete process mining workflow.

Functionality Overview


Get a selection of classical ProM plugins in KNIME
Create Large-scale and repetitive experiments. Process mining experiments in the analytical workflow context can be executed with just one click.
Create your process analysis comfortably in a visual workflow environment
Discover process models from your event data
Effortlessly use other KNIME extensions to further preprocess and post-process the data for process mining algorithms.
Save your current analysis, with its configuration and input as KNIME workflows, and share it with others!
